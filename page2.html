Interest

I have always believed in the strong potential of machine vision, both in terms of artistic creation and the future of the technology. I want to open up the limits of human senses so that people can reach any corner of the world anywhere, anytime. Movement and information gathering are the most critical parts, and currently Jetnano can provide enough algorithmic support for our robots, while Arzure kinect DK can provide good deep vision and development potential. On the other hand, in order to catch up with the market demand, FPGAs including Xilinx and mainstream embedded and Raspberry Pi are being tested..
